The models use URP shaders by default.
If you work in another render pipeline, use one of the included .unitypackage files to automatically convert all materials to the correct shader type.

Available packages (located in Assets/Render_Pipeline_Convert/):
- "URP_Materials_source" — converts all materials to URP.
- "HDRP_Materials_source" — converts all materials to HDRP.
- "Built-In_Materials_source" — converts all materials to Built-In.

How to use:
1. Open your project in Unity.
2. Double-click the required .unitypackage.
3. Press Import.

After import, all materials in the project will use shaders for the selected pipeline.
